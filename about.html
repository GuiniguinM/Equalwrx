About EqualWorx

Our Mission

EqualWorx is dedicated to creating equal employment opportunities for individuals with disabilities. We believe in building a more inclusive workforce where talent and ability are the only metrics that matter.

What We Do

We connect talented individuals with disabilities to employers who value diversity and inclusion. Our platform provides:

  • Accessible job search and application process
  • Customized profile creation
  • Skills-based job matching
  • Support throughout the employment journey
