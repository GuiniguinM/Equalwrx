Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a profile? ▼
To create a profile, go to Profile Information in Settings. Fill in your personal details, qualifications, and work preferences. Don't forget to save your changes.
How can I search for jobs? ▼
Use the search bar on the home page to look for specific jobs, or browse through our categories to find jobs that match your interests and qualifications.
How do I update my resume? ▼
Go to Resume in Settings. You can edit any section by clicking on it. Don't forget to click the Save button after making changes.
